Compliance

NexPay is designed with compliance-aware flows for KYC, AML, regional rules and issuer approvals.

Regulatory Readiness

Card access depends on applicable laws, supported regions, partner rules and financial regulations.

KYC Review

Identity checks are required before real card issuing, especially for regulated payment products.

Issuer Approval

Financial services require licensed infrastructure, program managers and approved card issuing partners.

Restricted Use

Some categories, regions, users or transactions may be restricted due to compliance requirements.

AML Controls

Anti-money laundering checks and risk screening are essential for real-world crypto card programs.

Partner Review

Business/API access should be reviewed before onboarding to protect users and platform partners.
