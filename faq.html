Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about NexPay

What is NexPay?
NexPay is a crypto card platform that allows users to use virtual cards for online payments.
How do I create a card?
First create an account, then choose a plan from pricing. Your card will be available in dashboard.
Can I add funds?
Yes, you can add funds from dashboard using the Add Funds button.
Is NexPay secure?
NexPay is designed with secure architecture, spending controls, and compliance-ready systems.
Do you support physical cards?
Physical cards are currently under development and will be available soon.
